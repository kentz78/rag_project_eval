# Employee Handbook — Acme Robotics Pte Ltd

## 1. Annual Leave

Full-time employees are entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave per calendar year, accrued monthly at a rate of 1.5 days per month. Annual leave entitlement increases by 1 day for every completed year of service, up to a maximum of 24 days.

Part-time employees accrue annual leave on a pro-rated basis according to their contracted hours.

Unused annual leave may be carried forward into the following year, up to a maximum of 5 days. Any leave in excess of 5 days that remains unused by 31 March of the following year is forfeited, except where carry-forward was blocked by business needs at management's request.

Leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 3 working days in advance for requests of 2 days or fewer, and at least 2 weeks in advance for requests of 3 days or more. Approval is at the discretion of the employee's direct manager.

## 2. Sick Leave

Employees are entitled to 14 days of paid outpatient sick leave and 60 days of paid hospitalisation leave per calendar year, inclusive of the 14 outpatient days, in line with the Singapore Ministry of Manpower's Employment Act guidelines. A medical certificate (MC) from a registered medical practitioner is required for any sick leave of 1 day or more.

## 3. Working Hours

Standard working hours are 9:00am to 6:00pm, Monday to Friday, with a 1-hour lunch break, totaling 40 hours per week. Employees on flexible work arrangements may shift their start time between 8:00am and 10:00am with their manager's agreement, provided core hours of 10:00am–4:00pm are maintained.

## 4. Probation

New employees serve a probation period of 3 months from their start date. Confirmation of employment is subject to a satisfactory performance review at the end of the probation period. The probation period may be extended by up to 3 months at management's discretion if performance is inconclusive.

## 5. Public Holidays

Employees are entitled to all gazetted public holidays in Singapore. If a public holiday falls on a Saturday or Sunday, the following Monday is observed as a paid holiday in lieu.

## 6. Parental Leave

Eligible employees are entitled to statutory maternity leave (16 weeks) and paternity leave (2 weeks) in accordance with the Child Development Co-Savings Act. Employees should notify HR and their manager at least 8 weeks before the expected leave start date.

## 7. Benefits

- Group hospitalisation and surgical insurance for all confirmed employees, extendable to dependents at the employee's cost.
- Annual outpatient medical claim of up to SGD 500.
- Dental claim of up to SGD 300 per year.
- Learning and development budget of SGD 1,000 per employee per year, subject to manager approval.

## 8. Resignation and Notice Period

Employees on probation must provide 2 weeks' written notice to resign. Confirmed employees must provide 1 month's written notice, unless a longer period is specified in their individual employment contract. The company reserves the right to accept payment in lieu of notice.
