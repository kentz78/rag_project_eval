# Frequently Asked Questions — Acme Robotics Pte Ltd

**Q: How much PTO do I get?**
See the Annual Leave section of the Employee Handbook — full-time employees start at 18 days per year, increasing with tenure up to 24 days.

**Q: Who do I contact if I'm sick and can't come in?**
Notify your manager as early as possible on the day of absence, and submit your medical certificate through the HR portal within 3 working days.

**Q: Can I work from home every day?**
The default arrangement is hybrid (3 office days, 2 remote days). Fully remote arrangements exist but require director-level approval — see the Remote Work Policy.

**Q: How do I claim travel expenses?**
Submit an itemised receipt through the finance portal within 30 days of the expense. See the Expense and Travel Policy for approval limits and per diem rates.

**Q: What's the notice period if I resign?**
2 weeks during probation, 1 month once confirmed, unless your contract states otherwise.

**Q: Does the company match retirement contributions?**
The company contributes to CPF in accordance with statutory requirements. Acme Robotics does not currently offer contributions beyond statutory CPF.

**Q: Can I expense alcohol?**
Only as part of an approved client entertainment event, and it must be itemised with attendee names and business purpose.

**Q: I'm going on parental leave — who do I tell and when?**
Notify HR and your manager at least 8 weeks before your expected leave start date.

**Q: What happens to unused annual leave at year end?**
Up to 5 days can be carried into the next year; anything beyond that is forfeited by 31 March unless carry-forward was blocked by business needs.

**Q: Can I use my personal laptop to check work email?**
Only via the approved mobile email app. Accessing other company systems from personal devices is not permitted.
