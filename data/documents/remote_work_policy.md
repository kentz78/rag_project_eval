# Remote Work Policy — Acme Robotics Pte Ltd

## 1. Eligibility

Employees who have completed their probation period and whose role does not require physical presence (e.g., lab work, hardware assembly, on-site client support) are eligible to apply for a remote work arrangement.

## 2. Hybrid Default

The default working arrangement is hybrid: 3 days per week in the office (Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday are designated anchor days) and up to 2 days working remotely, subject to manager approval.

## 3. Fully Remote Arrangements

Fully remote arrangements (0 days in office) require director-level approval and are reviewed every 6 months. Employees approved for fully remote work must be contactable during core hours (10:00am–4:00pm Singapore time) regardless of their physical location.

## 4. Equipment

The company provides a laptop, monitor, and a one-time home office setup allowance of SGD 400 for employees on an approved remote or hybrid arrangement. Equipment remains company property and must be returned upon termination of employment.

## 5. Working from Overseas

Employees wishing to work remotely from outside Singapore for more than 10 consecutive working days must obtain prior written approval from HR and their manager, due to tax, payroll, and work-pass implications. Unapproved overseas remote work may affect payroll processing and insurance coverage.

## 6. Data Security

Remote work must be conducted on company-issued devices with VPN and disk encryption enabled. Use of personal devices to access company systems is prohibited except for email via the approved mobile app.

## 7. Review and Revocation

Remote work privileges are not contractual and may be adjusted or revoked by management at any time based on business needs, team performance, or role changes.
